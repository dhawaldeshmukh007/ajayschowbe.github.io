Professional Profile

Dr. Ajay Shrikrishna Chowbe is a seasoned educationist, school leader, and academic administrator with over 28 years of distinguished experience in CBSE and ICSE institutions across India. Known for his strategic vision, ethical leadership, and deep understanding of academic governance, he has consistently contributed to institutional growth, teacher empowerment, and student success.

His professional journey reflects a strong commitment to quality education, values-based learning, and continuous improvement, aligned with national education reforms and global best practices.

Educational Philosophy

Dr. Chowbe firmly believes that education must go beyond academic achievement to nurture critical thinking, character development, leadership skills, and social responsibility. His approach integrates scholastic excellence with co-curricular enrichment, emotional well-being, and ethical grounding.

He advocates for a student-centric, competency-based learning environment, in alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP 2020), where teachers act as facilitators, mentors, and lifelong learners.

Leadership & Administrative Expertise

  • Strategic academic planning and institutional development
  • Implementation of NEP 2020 and CBSE competency-based curriculum
  • Faculty recruitment, mentoring, appraisal, and professional development
  • Academic audits, inspections, and statutory compliance
  • Policy formulation, SOP development, and governance frameworks
  • Parent-school partnership and stakeholder engagement

CBSE Inspection & Advisory Experience

Dr. Chowbe has been appointed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi as a Member of Inspection Committee for inspection of schools seeking CBSE affiliation and upgradation to senior secondary level.

In this role, he has contributed expert evaluation on infrastructure norms, academic processes, staff qualifications, safety standards, and governance mechanisms. His work is recognized for its professionalism, objectivity, and adherence to CBSE regulations.

Key Achievements & Contributions

  • Established and stabilized new schools as Founder Principal
  • Implemented structured teacher training and mentoring systems
  • Recognized at regional and national levels for academic leadership

Personal Note

Beyond administrative responsibilities, Dr. Chowbe is deeply passionate about mentoring educators, engaging with academic communities, and contributing to educational reform initiatives.
